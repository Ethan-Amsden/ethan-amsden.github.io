Don's Medieval Fantasy

As I grew up as, I had a love for medieval fantasy. When my cousins came over, we would always play games that involved an entire worlds of castles, knights, rangers, dragons, and the like.

Travel with Red the Stick Figure as he journeys across the Forsaken Plains and through the treacherous Orkusbloorg Mountains to defeat the Titan Griffin that has been terrorizing his baron's territory! But watch out! The treacherous Orkusbloorg Mountains are controlled by the evil Cave Orks who show no mercy!

Travel with Red the Stick Figure as he searches for the The legendary Black Sword! When he reaches the the sword he has to face a challenger, but does a friendship bloom?

Travel with Red the Stick Figure as his friend as they search for the Fogotten Library to find information about the orks who destroyed their home villages.
