About
Don Spencer

Don's Bibliography

Don Spencer began his working life as software engineer before being promoted to project director at his job at Chameleon Games. After 20 years at Chameleon Games Don moved on to partner with his friend Joe Bartner in boardgame building. While building boardgames with his friend, Don started to write fiction to encouragehis and Joe's Children's imagination.

Don lives in the cliffside beach suburb of Emerald located on Ember Island of Australia, and is currently writing more titles in the The Adventures of Red the Stick Figure series and is starting a new series, Galactic Warfare.

Don's Writing

I use to draw stick figures a lot as a child, especially red stick figures. Now that I look back, I noticed that I had a weird obsession for red stick figures. Whenever Joe and I needed to make some character tokens for testing our board games, I would draw some colored stick figures on craft paper or cardboard and fold them in such a way to make them stand up vertically. A lot of the games we made were medieval games that had somewhat of a story line to it. That inspired me to start writing fiction books based off of some of the story lines we made for our boardgames.

I though it would be unique to write a fiction book with stick figures instead of read people. After I saw how well that book sold, I decided to write more books to create a whole series. As time went on, people asked me if I was interested in writing space fiction. I told them that I would give it a try, even though I enjoyed writing medieval fiction more. In doing so, I kept one character for my readers to follow throughout all my book series.

~ Don Spencer
