Floating Treasure Island

Dear Visitor,

Renowned for its altitude, this mountain holds a secret treasure deep within, no one has found it, but there are records nearby that tell of wealth hidden within. But the mountain is full of dangerous traps and monsters. Don't believe me, I've witnessed it for myself alongside many others. But watch out! the pirates too are seeking the treasure and are willing to do anything to beat you to it!

Now, if you think you are ready to find the hidden treasure, think again, many legends have tried and met their fate! You need to go through some rigorous training under Lord Gordon to even have a chance at this treasure.

Be careful out there,
