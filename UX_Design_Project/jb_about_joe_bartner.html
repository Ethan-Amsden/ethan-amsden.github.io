About Joe Bartner

Joe's Bibliography

Joe Bartner began his working life as Steam Engine Driver for Sir Hamilton's Railway. After 31 years at Sir Hamilton's Railway, Joe moved on to partner with his friend Don Snarfbarger in boardgame building. While building boardgames with his friend, Joe decided it was time to build a website (this website) for their latest boardgame to encourage distant friends and family to explore the game and share it with others.

Joe lives in the cliffside beach suburb of Emerald located on Ember Island of Australia, and is currently working on the boardgame, Treasure Pirates, plus an educational railway videogame (not yet named).
